This is /src/lib/chain_process/Chain.cpp and Chain.h's description:

Contain the Chain class. Containing a vector of Note objects, score, a vector "cont" that signifies the continuity of
our Notes (for example, Notes with indices 1 2 5 6 will be with cont 1 0 1). Also contains a match method- comparing its
vector of Notes to another vector of Notes (from image).
